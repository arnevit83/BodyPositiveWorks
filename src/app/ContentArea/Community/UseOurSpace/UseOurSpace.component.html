Use Our Space


Interested in holding a meeting or event in our space? Our home is a beautiful heritage house in the heart of Saddle River, Bergen County. We love sharing it with local businesses and individuals wanting to hold a meeting or event.

We are centrally located with convenient access to Rt. 17 and the Garden State Parkway, and on the border of Rockland County. We are directly across the street from Wandell Elementary School, and next door to the Municipal Building.

Times must of course be pre-arranged with us and mutually agreed upon. We can show you around and discuss what space best meets your needs. Please arrange an appointment to take a tour.
