What is Body Therapy?


In our Body Therapy sessions, we use a technique called SourcePoint Therapy®. The starting point of SourcePoint is that pain can only be healed from the inside out.

Like a human MRI machine, the practitioner “reads” bodies and makes all kinds of (sometimes surprising) connections between bones, muscles, tissues and organs.

The body is looked at as a whole… not broken up into parts as is the more conventional way of seeing things. For example, if you have a knee issue a doctor may say you need surgery, whereas the SourcePoint practitioner may find that the source of the knee issue is located elsewhere in the body. Similarly, bunions could be an example of something going on in the kidney.

The idea is to locate energetic blockages that obstruct the so-called “Blueprint of Health” – the information of natural order, balance, harmony, and flow inherent in the body and in the universal energy of Source.

This is a simple, non-invasive energetic approach to healing; you will remain fully clothed throughout.

Benefits of BodyTherapy


The benefits of Body Therapy are many – it works on the body to address aches and pains, health issues, feelings of imbalance or psychological concerns. There is an improved experience of the mind/body connection, the relief of tension, anxiety and stress, better sleep, better circulation, pain relief, increased mobility and a better quality of life.

One of the major benefits of SourcePoint Therapy® is that it’s very empowering – through it you see that, far from being a “black box”, you do have a lot of control over the things that go on in the body.

Jody (Domerstad) Boisits
Jody (Domerstad) Boisits is a seasoned educator in body, mind, philosophy, and healthful living. She feels strongly, as an echo of her own healing journey, that no one should suffer.

Jody’s experience with some of this life’s more serious threats including addiction, PTSD, a brain tumor, and infectious disease has necessarily deepened her understanding of how a comprehensive yoga practice - including philosophy, asana, breathing & meditation - can help to understand and overcome those challenges.

The positive and lasting effects of yoga were so life changing for her that she has dedicated her life to sharing what she’s learned so others can benefit.

With a masterful knowledge of alignment, a keen eye for detail, and laser precision with how to deliver cues to take students to a peak asana practice, Jody’s asana students and fellow teachers alike have deemed her “the teacher of teachers.”

Since undergoing brain surgery Jody’s studies have shifted to yin yoga with founder Paul Grilley - 200hr TT - and continuing study with Sarah Powers - Yin Level I, II & more. Additionally, she has completed multiple restorative yoga trainings with Judith Lassater and Jillian Pranksy.

Earlier studies include: 500 ERYT and multiple 200 hour trainings in Vinyasa, Alignment based – Hatha and The Barkan Method.

Tertiary wellness trainings include: Inner Light Method, Neurosculpting® Facilitator, SourcePoint Therapy®, Reiki Master & Spiritual Response Therapy practitioner.

Jody’s yoga path includes a 500 ERYT, studying with the best of the best teachers in an impressive array of specialty yoga, over a decade of study of alignment-based hatha, and so much more (including Inner Light Method, Neurosculpting®, SourcePoint Therapy®).
