What is a Chiropractor?


A chiropractor helps relieve problems with bones, muscles and joints, enabling the smooth running of the nervous system.

This natural form of healthcare uses gentle spinal adjustments to correct misalignments and restore proper function to the nervous system. A chiropractor applies precise movements to specific parts of the spinal segment to correct misalignments, permitting enhanced nerve transmission and assisting the body’s ability to recover, without drugs or surgery.

Regular sessions with a Chiropractor will help keep your body tuned and well – no need to save it for when you’ve hurt yourself!

Benefits of a Chiropractic Treatment


Chiropractic is the largest drug-free profession and third largest primary health care profession in the world. Most people seek out a chiropractor because of pain, and continue care because they experience a better quality of life and enhanced health – with their nervous system at optimal potential.

Clinical studies show that chiropractic care can help reduce pain better than many medications. It can assist in the relief of issues like sciatica, neck pain, scoliosis, lower back pain, headaches, and frozen shoulder.
