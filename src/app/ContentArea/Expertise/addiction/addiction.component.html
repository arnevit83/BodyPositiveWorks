What is Addiction?


The textbook definition of addiction is the psychological and physical inability to stop consuming a chemical, drug, activity, or substance, even when it’s causing psychological and physical harm.

At Body Positive Works we dig deeper. We go way beyond, seeking to understand the reasons behind the individual’s addiction – exploring relationships with family, friends, and most importantly – relationship with the self.

That’s why we identify and address the heart and mind issues behind the addiction, walking alongside the individual on their journey to short term stabilization, and long-term growth, health and wellness.

Why Choose Us?


Our drug addiction treatment, along with our alcohol addiction treatment, is holistic; we take a mind/body approach to supporting people struggling with an alcohol or drug addiction, and their families.

Body Positive Works is proud to offer NJ drug and alcohol addiction help. Our Co-Founder, Melanie Struble, is a Certified Drug & Alcohol Counselor. Along with her business partner Jen Kraft, she created Body Positive Works to bring everything together under one roof, providing expert practitioners in every discipline – from psychotherapy to nutrition to yoga and meditation – to support individuals on their journey to recovery.
