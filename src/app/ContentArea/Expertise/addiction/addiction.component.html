What Is Addiction?



The textbook definition of addiction is the psychological and physical inability to stop consuming a chemical, drug, activity, or substance, even when it’s causing psychological and physical harm.

At Body Positive Works we dig deeper. We go way beyond, seeking to understand the reasons behind the individual’s addiction – exploring our relationships with our family, friends, and most importantly – our relationship with ourselves.

That’s why we identify and address the heart and mind issues behind the addict’s relationship with their addiction, walking alongside them on their journey to short term stabilisation, and long term growth, health and wellness.

Why Choose Us?



Our approach to addiction is holistic. Body Positive Works is a one-stop shop offering integrated services, programs, and classes to support individuals on their journey to recovery. We’ve brought everything together under one roof, providing best in class practitioners from every discipline – from psychotherapy to nutrition to yoga and meditation.
