Addiction Therapists NJ

Body Positive Works are addiction specialists serving NJ, Bergen County and Rockland County.

Our approach to addiction is holistic. Body Positive Works is a one-stop shop offering integrated addiction help (in particular drug addiction help) in the form of services, programs, and classes to support individuals on their journey to recovery.





What Is Addiction?



The textbook definition of addiction is the psychological and physical inability to stop consuming a chemical, drug, activity, or substance, even when it’s causing psychological and physical harm.

At Body Positive Works we dig deeper. We go way beyond, seeking to understand the reasons behind the individual’s addiction – exploring our relationships with our family, friends, and most importantly – our relationship with ourselves.

That’s why we identify and address the heart and mind issues behind the addict’s relationship with their addiction, walking alongside them on their journey to short term stabilisation, and long term growth, health and wellness.



Why Choose Us?



Our approach to addiction is holistic. Body Positive Works is a one-stop shop offering integrated services, programs, and classes to support individuals on their journey to recovery. We’ve brought everything together under one roof, providing best in class practitioners from every discipline – from psychotherapy to nutrition to yoga and meditation.

Irene Maltzan Chac
Melanie, co-founder of Body Positive Works, is a psychotherapist whose practice is dedicated to working with adolescents, adults, and families who are dealing with eating disorders, body image, substance , and stage of life issues. A Licensed Clinical Social Worker and Licensed Clinical Alcohol and Drug Counselor with 25 years of direct practice experience, she holds the highest New Jersey licensure in both disciplines.

Over the years, Melanie has worked with clients in a variety of settings: hospital, crisis center, private non-profit agency, and private practice. She has gained a broad perspective and deep understanding of the challenges her clients face when seeking specialized services from multiple resources. The Body Positive Works was born of the realization that having a variety of services under one roof – and providing a strong sense of community – would support clients in achieving greater overall health, recovery, and long-term growth.

Melanie holds a Masters of Social Work from Columbia University and a BA in Psychology and Law and Justice from The College of New Jersey.
